Massachusetts Paid Family and Medical Leave
    Client Screening App



    LAWYERING IN THE AGE OF SMART MACHINES | PROJECT PROPOSAL

    • The deliverable

      A Docassemble app designed for Prosody Law that provides the firm with a guided user interview to determine if the user qualifies as an aggreived employee under the Massachusetts Paid Family and Medical Leave Act (PFML).

      Try it out (Coming Soon)

    The client

    WHO: The client must be a current or former employee who has been aggrieved under PMFL. This includes employees that have been retaliated against by being:

    • check_circle Discharged

      check_circle Fired

      check_circle Suspended

      check_circle Expelled

      check_circle Disciplined

    GOAL: The goal will be to inform the client on whether they qualify as an aggrieved employee and if so, provide a completed client screening form, contact information for Prosody Law, and a checklist of relevant documents.

    • The app

    The app will ask clients questions across three categories to determine if they qualify:

    • Employment Information

        check Do they meet the statutory definition of "employee"?

        check Does their employer meet the statutory definition of "employer"?

        check Employment status (part time, full time)

        check Salary/hourly wage

        checkHow they were aggrieved

    • Biographical Information

        check First and last name

        check Primary language

        check Contact information (email, phone number)

        check Location
