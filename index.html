    Under the hood

    The app combines three components to power its usability:

    • Guided Interview

        check Asks users a series of questions to determine if a data breach needs reporting

        check Directs users to the MA Office of Consumer Affairs and Business Regulation reporting form

        check Directs users to the MA Office of Attorney General reporting form

        checkOptional page to collect information required for reporting

    • Document Assembly

        check Downloadable firm-branded memorandum in Word format

        check Populates memorandum with statutory definitions and full text of applicable statutes relevant to user input

        check Populates summary of data breach information from user input

        check Populates the MA Office of Attorney General template reporting letter with collected user input
        (coming soon!)

    • Integrated Database

        check Custom variables parsing definitions, statutes, and data breach incident reporting

        check Robust and flexible editing capabilities of master spreadsheet in Airtable

        check Exportable in .xlsx for easy convertability into Excel
